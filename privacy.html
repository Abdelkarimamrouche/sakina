What is Sakina

Sakina is a Chrome Extension that uses on-device AI (YAMNet via TensorFlow.js) to automatically detect and mute music in YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok videos â while preserving speech, Quran recitation, and ambient sound.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Sakina Chrome Extension available on the Chrome Web Store.

Data we collect

We collect nothing. Sakina operates entirely on your device.

  • No audio is recorded or stored at any point.
  • No browsing history or watch history is accessed.
  • No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • No analytics or telemetry is sent to external services.
  • No account or registration is required.

How the AI works

Sakina uses YAMNet bundled within the extension. Runs entirely in your browser. No cloud inference.

Quran recitation is protected. Sakina applies an instrument-detection gate. Audio without musical instruments is never muted.
  • Audio frames are processed and immediately discarded.
  • The model does not learn from your usage.
  • Zero network activity during classification.
Third-party services

Sakina does not integrate with any analytics or tracking service.

The popup loads fonts from Google Fonts. No audio or personal data is involved.
